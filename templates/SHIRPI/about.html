About SHIRPI

SHIRPI was created by Kyle Ivey and Nathan Magnus as a term project for CS215 at the University of Regina

The goal of this project was to improve upon a the Government of Saskatchewan Ministry of Health's website by adding important functionality that it lacked as well as a community aspect. This data presented is publically available in its original form at http://orii.health.gov.sk.ca/.
