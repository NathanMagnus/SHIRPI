Disclaimer

It is essential that all users of this web application understand how flawed its metrics are.

For example, the locations rated as being the absolute worst by our weights, still have only received 'low' re-inspection priorities according to the inspector. Our weights are based on our best interpretations of the how influential a given 'item' mentioned in a report would make us disinterested in eating there.

The data provided in the reports is both limited and biased. Item number 14 for example, "All locations must be free of vermin" could have been noted due to something as benign as an uncleaned flytrap or a dead moth stuck in a light fixture, or potentially something as horrible as a mummified cat in the ceiling - the reports do not specify.

Do not use these reports and our interpretations as the single deciding factor into whether you will patron an establishment

About SHIRPI

SHIRPI was created by Kyle Ivey and Nathan Magnus as a term project for CS215 at the University of Regina

The goal of this project was to improve upon a the Government of Saskatchewan Ministry of Health's website by adding important functionality that it lacked as well as a community aspect. This data presented is publicly available in its original form at orii via Saskhealth.

API

	An API to the logs is available here. It uses get requests identical to shirpi/browse but instead outputs in XML.
	
	The only specail parameter it accepts is ?display=full, which will output the actuall logs within search results.
	
	Examples:
		List all McDonalds in the city:
		   http://basementbobs.com/cs215/shirpi/api?restaurant_name=mcdonalds
		
		List all locations on Victoria street:
		   http://basementbobs.com/cs215/shirpi/browse?restaurant_address=vic
		
		Display Reports for all McDonalds on Victora:
		   http://basementbobs.com/cs215/shirpi/browse?estaurant_name=mcdonalds&restaurant_address=vic&display=full
